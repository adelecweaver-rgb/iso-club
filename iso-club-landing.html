Questions

The honest answers.

Do I need to be fit to start? ▼
No. Dustin designs protocols for where you are right now, not where you think you should be. Many of our members started completely sedentary. The machines adapt to your output — you can never "do it wrong."
How long are the sessions? ▼
Most training sessions are 20–30 minutes. Recovery sessions run 30–60 minutes. The CAROL REHIT protocol is just 8 minutes. Iso Club is built for people who want maximum results in minimum time.
What if I don't have a Whoop or Oura? ▼
Wearables are optional — not required. You get full value from Iso Club without one. If you have a wearable it enhances what Dustin can see and how precisely he can adjust your protocol, but the in-studio data alone is powerful.
How often do I need to come in? ▼
Dustin designs most protocols around 3 sessions per week. Some members come in more for recovery modalities. Because the machines are so efficient, 3 sessions of 20–30 minutes each produces results most people can't achieve with 5 hours of traditional training.
What's the difference between the tiers? ▼
Every tier gets the same machines, the same Dustin protocol, and the same Healthspan OS dashboard. The tiers differ in how much direct access to Dustin you have — messaging, 1:1 reviews, scan analysis, and reports. If you want the data and the protocol without needing to talk to Dustin often, Essential works perfectly.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. We think the results speak for themselves — if you're not seeing measurable progress in your Healthspan OS scores, we want to know why and fix it, not lock you in.
